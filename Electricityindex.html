Electricity Info

Basic Definitions

  • Voltage (V): Electrical potential difference, in volts.
  • Current (I): Flow of electric charge, in amperes (A).
  • Resistance (R): Opposition to current flow, in ohms (Ω).
  • Power (P): Rate of energy usage, in watts (W).
  • Energy (E): Electricity consumed, in kilowatt-hours (kWh).

Formulas

V = I × R (Ohm’s Law)

P = V × I (Power)

E = P × t (Energy)
